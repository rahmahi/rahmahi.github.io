About

I am a theoretical physicist specializing in quantum statistical mechanics and many-body physics. My research focuses on the intersection of quantum localization phenomena, periodically driven systems, and exotic phases of matter. I work extensively with tensor network methods, quantum simulation, and computational approaches to understand complex quantum systems. Currently, I am exploring quantum neural networks and their applications in many-body physics, alongside investigating novel phenomena such as quantum scars, time crystals, and quantum chimera states.
